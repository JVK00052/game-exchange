Need Help?

Please check out our FAQ section, and if you do not see that your question has already been answered previously, feel free to contact us at 1-800-NAI-LDIT, or complete our contact form found on the previous page. Our hours of operation are Monday-Friday 8a-6p, Saturday 10a-6p, and Sunday 10a-4p.


Q: I am unsatisfied with my purchase and the product I bought. How do I refund my purchase?

A: Please contact us via email or telephone within 30 days of purchase and we can help you right away with a full refund.


Q: The product shipped to us was in poor condition, how do I mail it back?

A: Our shipping address is 12175 Visionary Way, Fisher In, 46038.


Q: Is it possible to put in special order requests?

A: Yes! If you have a specific product or item that our online store does not have, please email Naildit@business.inquiries.com for further information.


Q: One of the items you have is "out of stock", how long will it take for your store to restock it's inventory?

A: We're very sorry for this inconvenience, it usually takes 3-5 business days in order to refill our stock.


Q: Where do you get your products from? Are they from safe and trusted companies?

A: Yes! All of our products come from well renowned companies such as Metal Masters, Armoured Drills, The Canadian Bolt, and ScrewTapes INC.


Q: I ordered products from Nail'd It, but why hasn't my packaged arrived yet?

A: Due to how small our company is, it may take from 4-7 business days in order to arrive due to location and distance.


Q: My package got lost in the mail, do I qualify for a refund?

A: Of course! Please contact us immediately via email for further information.


Q: Found an error?

A: Report it to us at Naildit@business.errors.com and we will fix it right away!


Q: How do I file a formal complaint?

A: Please call the help line and press 3. An online representative will get to you as soon as possible.
